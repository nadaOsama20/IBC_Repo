ESOL

LanguageCert International ESOL (English for Speakers of Other Languages) is a suite of English language qualifications specially designed for those intending to study, work or travel around the globe.

SELT / UKVI

A fast, reliable and friendly way of certifying English language skills to UKVI requirements. LanguageCert offers UK Home Office approved SELT exams for UK Visas & Immigration (UKVI) worldwide, covering all visa type requirements to live, work or study in the UK.

LTE

The English listening and reading test that is in line with (A1-C2) levels by CEFR, is ideal for middle to advanced level candidates (B2-C2). Exams are offered through private global testing centers or online. Used in jobs, work and promotion.

YOUNG LEARNER

is a set of English language qualifications at introductory levels, specifically created for younger candidates (aged 7+), aiming to motivate them to improve their English skills and help them become familiar with the examination process for their future endeavors.
